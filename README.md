### **About Me**

- **Current Role:** PhD Scholar in Computer Science & Engineering at **National Institute of Technology Arunachal Pradesh**.
- **Core Focus:** Bridging the linguistic barrier for low-resource languages, with a deep passion for the **Tani language family** (Tagin, Nyishi, Adi, Apatani, Galo and Mising).
- **Research & Endeavors:** 
  - Developing **Neural Machine Translation (NMT)** and **Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)** systems for unrepresented languages.
  - Building the first **Masked Language Modelling (MLM)**, **NMT Models**, **ASR Models**, **Embeddings**, and **Part-of-Speech (POS) Tagger** for all the Tanyi languages (Tagin, Nyishi, Adi, Apatani, Galo and Mising).
- **Open Source Contributions:** 
  - Managing parallel datasets like **GinLishCorpus** and **Tagin-POS-Corpus**.
  - Building and open-sourcing tools such as the **TaginBERT**, **NyishiBERT**, **GaloBERT**, and **ApataniBERT**.
  - Releasing pre-trained NMT models like `eng-tagin-nmt`, `eng-nyi-nmt`, and `eng-taw-nmt` on Hugging Face.
- **App Development:** Deploying full-stack translator applications leveraging Streamlit, Huggingface Transformers, PyTorch and Python.
